  /getaccount
    Claim a free account from the pool.
    • Normal mode  — 1 account, 12-hour cooldown
    • Party mode   — 1–10 random accounts, 2-second cooldown
    • Unlimited    — no cooldown (any mode)
    Account is sent privately to your DMs.

  /poolstatus
    Shows how many accounts are left in the pool
    and how many have been given out total.

  /invite
    Generates your personal invite link and sends
    it to your DMs. Share it with new members.

  /claimbonus
    After clicking someone's invite link and joining
    the channel, run this to claim your 5 free accounts.
    The person who invited you also gets 5 accounts.

  /unlimited
    Toggle cooldown on/off for /getaccount.
    ON  — users can claim with zero wait time.
    OFF — normal cooldown restored (12 hrs / 2 secs).

  /party
    Toggle party mode for /getaccount.
    ON  — users get 1–10 random accounts + public shoutout in chat.
          Cooldown drops to 2 seconds.
    OFF — back to 1 account per claim, 12-hour cooldown.

  /inviteinfo
    Posts a formatted explanation of the invite system
    (rewards, rules, how-to) to the announcement channel.

  /extrafromchange
    One-time command. Reads the accounts log, finds every
    user who received an invite bonus under the old 2-account
    system, and DMs them 3 extra accounts to make up the
    difference to the new 5-account reward.

   INVITE SYSTEM — QUICK REFERENCE
  ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  1. Admin or user runs /invite  →  bot DMs them their link
  2. They share the link with a new member
  3. New member clicks link  →  opens bot, invite is registered
  4. New member joins @TnnrCPM channel
  5. New member runs /claimbonus
     →  New member gets 5 accounts
     →  Inviter gets 5 accounts

  Rules:
    • New member must be joining @TnnrCPM for the first time
    • Each person can only claim the invite bonus once
    • You cannot use your own invite link
    • All accounts are delivered via DM
